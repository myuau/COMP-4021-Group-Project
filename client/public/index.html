Instruction

You are a chef in the McDonald.

Customers will place orders, which will appear at the bottom of the. Each order shows the required food items (e.g., hamburger, fries, drink).

You need to collect and submit the correct ingredients for making the food items in the order.

If you fail to complete an order within 20 seconds, it will expire and be removed.

Controls:

Move your character around the restaurant with:
UP
LEFT
DOWN
RIGHT
Collect or drop ingredients with:
E

When the game time ends, the player with the highest total income wins! If players have the same income, the game is declared a tie.

Press Spacebar to activate Cheat Mode~ Your character will move faster to collect ingredients and complete orders much more quickly.
